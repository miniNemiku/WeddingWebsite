Our Story

April 2016

How We Met

We met on a rainy Tuesday at a local coffee shop. Sarah was working on her laptop when John accidentally spilled his coffee nearby. What started as an awkward apology turned into a two-hour conversation about everything from travel dreams to favorite books.

February 2020

First Date

Our first official date was at the botanical gardens. We walked for hours among exotic plants, getting to know each other and discovering our shared love for nature and photography.

July 2023

The Proposal

After hiking to the summit of Mount Rainier at sunrise, John got down on one knee with a ring and a promise. The view was breathtaking, but Sarah says she barely noticed it through happy tears.

August 2025

Our Wedding Day

And now, we can't wait to begin the next chapter of our story with all of you by our side.
